## Description 
Activities in the Triad is a local activity and booking platform designed for the Piedmont Triad area. It helps people who are looking for things to do. 

There are many experiences across the Triad. But customers waste time searching, miss out on things they're interested in, and settle for staying home.

Our goal is to create an easy user friendly platform where users can discover and book local activities. This will save users time, find activities that match their interests, time, and budget. For providers, it's a great way to attract new customers, list their activities, and grow their business.
